Winter at the Beach

As a kid one of my favorite wintertime activities was to go to the beach. Bundled up in warm coats and my mom’s shawls my siblings and I would gather around the sizzling BBQ, as the aroma of chicken kebabs and burgers filled the air. The waves would crash at the shore, creating a loud but somehow soothing melody. Going to the beach in the winter just felt so different than going there in the summer, everything felt different, even though it was the same place and the same people, the air was different. It felt calmer. Right before sunset, my sister and I would race to see who could toast the most marshmallows and eat the most s’mores. It was a magical experience and a memory that I will carry with me forever. Till this day when I visit the beach during the winter, I feel as if I have been transported back to that time and all the emotions come running back.
