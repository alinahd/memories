One Direction was the soundtrack of my childhood. It shaped my childhood and memories. Their songs sparked daydreams, sleepovers, sing-alongs, dance-offs. Their songs evoked various feelings and emotions in me, they provided me with comfort and inspiration.


From the upbeat meoldies of songs like "Best Song Ever" to the hearfelt ballad of "Little Things" I was obsessed with them and their songs. Over time, I was also able to start relating to the lyrics in the songs which formed a deeper connection between me and the music. They inspired me to embrace my individuality and to be confident.
