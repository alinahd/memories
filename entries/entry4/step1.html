Lets Get Started!

1. It's Saturday morning, I wake up and rush downstairs to the kitchen.


2. My mom’s recipe books are piled up on the counter waiting for me to explore and find a new recipe for the day.


3. I take a seat on the tall brown stool and start flipping through the books when I see the words “Oreo Cheesecake” written across the page I yell “found it!” and race to my mom to tell her.


4. My mom sits on the stool, and instructs me to first take out all the ingredients I need to make the cake.


5. This part, I hate, I run across the kitchen trying to carry as many things as possible so that I can start the fun part, the part where there’s batter all over my clothes and where the entire kitchen is filled with the aroma of vanilla essence.
